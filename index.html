Getting Started

  1. Open Capture and choose Front or Side.
  2. Adjust the onion skin slider to align with your previous photo.
  3. Tap the shutter. Optionally add weight and notes.
  4. Use Timeline to browse, long-press to delete.
  5. Use Compare for side-by-side or overlay comparisons.

Tip: You can enable a 3s/5s/10s timer from the control bar.

Features

  • Photos stored in-app files (not Camera Roll)
  • Front/Side poses with optional onion-skin alignment
  • Add weight & journal notes per entry
  • Timeline with long-press delete & full screen viewer
  • Compare: side-by-side or overlay with opacity slider
  • Flipbook export (choose start/end range)

FAQ

Where are my photos stored?

All photos are saved inside the app’s private storage on your device. They are not added to Photos unless you export them.

Do you upload my data?

No. Photos, weights, and notes stay on your device. If you delete the app, this local data is removed unless you’ve exported it.

How do I delete an entry?

On the Timeline, long-press a thumbnail and choose Delete Photo. This removes the photo and its associated weight/notes.

How do I export a flipbook video?

Open Timeline → Export Flipbook → select a start and end image → export.

Privacy Policy

The Body Journal stores photos, weights, and notes locally on your device. The app does not collect analytics, use third-party trackers, or send your content to external servers. Network access is only used for Apple services (e.g., TestFlight and the App Store). You can delete entries within the app at any time. To remove all data, delete the app from your device.
